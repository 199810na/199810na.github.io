The Sun Shines on the Earth

2022

Acrylic on Paper, Acrylic on Canvas

The Sun Shines on the Earth is a sequence of black and white acrylic paintings. The black color as the background on the canvas symbolizes the original state of the universe, which is also seen as the womb of the supreme goddess of creation. By combining traditional Chinese prehistoric fertility and reproduction symbols with everyday objects, the artist intends to represent the skeletal goddess confronting the external fertility oppression by society and awakening the internal power of women.
