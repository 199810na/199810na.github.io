Female Factory

2020

3D-mapping, Stop-motion Animation

Female Factory is a 3D projection installation. Stop-motion animations are projected on the front and back of three black and white TV sets, showing an automated factory that seems to solve the workplace discrimination toward women, but actually uses women's bodies to produce babies. In this process, women need to cycle through the three stages of eating, fertility, and aging. This endless loop forms a cage that women cannot escape from or breakthrough.
